About Us

Fintech Innovative is a venture studio specifically harnessing entrepreneurial talents in blockchain, artificial intelligence, machine learning and fintech for banking.

Fintech Innovative connects promising ideas run by great entrepreneurial talents to scale in conjunction with our corporate partners and larger ecosystem play. We partner with corporations, investors to leverage our experience and that hold potential for innovative technology to play a major disruptive role going forward. Fintech Innovative has a strong experience advisory team based out of Delhi. Fintech Innovative invests in early and growth-stage technology companies focused in India Markets. Our Vision is to use new age technology to reach out to unbanked region of India while supporting regional startups with appropriate mentoring and funding to scale to fit into a larger ecosystem play.
